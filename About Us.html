About Us

Welcome to SVS GUITAR, your dedicated hub for all things guitar, located right in the heart of Sampaloc, Manila. Our journey began with a simple passion: to bring the best in guitar craftsmanship, culture, and community right to your doorstep.


Founded by local musicians and guitar enthusiasts, SVS GUITAR was born out of a desire to fuse our love for music with the vibrant cultural tapestry of Manila. We believe that every guitar tells a story, and our mission is to help you find the instrument that speaks to you—whether you’re a beginner strumming your first chord or a seasoned pro seeking a unique piece.
